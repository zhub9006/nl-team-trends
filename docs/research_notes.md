# NL Team Trends Research Notes

## Methodology
Compiles NL historical performance data from multiple cross-referenced sources.

## Primary Sources
1. Baseball Almanac (baseball-almanac.com) - NL W-L matrices 1876-2026
2. Baseball-Reference (baseball-reference.com/leagues/NL/) - Official NL standings
3. SABR Lahman DB (sabr.org/lahman-database) - Free CSV 1871-2025
4. Baseball Data Hub (baseballdatahub.com) - Season archives 1871-2026
5. StatsCrew (statscrew.com/baseball/l-NL) - NL rosters and leaders
6. Linger & Look (lingerandlook.com) - Year-by-year standings

## Key Findings
- NL Founded: Feb 2, 1876 (8 teams)
- Original 8: BOS, CHI, CIN, HFD, LOU, PHI, BKN, STL
- Relocations: BKN->LA 1958, NYG->SF 1958, MTL->WAS 2005
- Schedule eras: Variable(1876-84), 154G(1892-1961), 162G(1962-2019), COVID 60G(2020)
- Most NL wins: SF Giants 11,663; STL Cardinals: 11 WS titles
- Best season: 1906 Cubs 116-36 (.763); Best 162G: 2022 LAD 111-51
- Braves dynasty: 14 straight division titles 1991-2005
- Dodgers dynasty: 9 pennants 5 WS since 2017; back-to-back 2024-25
- Cubs 2016: ended 108-year drought
