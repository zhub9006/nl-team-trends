# Data Notes & Methodology

## Scope
This repository compiles **National League (NL)** team performance data from **1876** (founding) through the **2025 season**.

## Data Conventions
- **Win-Loss Format**: Shown as `Wins-Losses` (e.g., 93-69)
- **Win Percentage**: Rounded to 3 decimal places (e.g., .535)
- **Schedule Length**:
  - 66 games (1876 inaugural season)
  - ~120-154 games (1877–1961)
  - 162 games (1961–present)
  - 60 games (2020, COVID-shortened)

## Important Historical Notes
- **Franchise relocations**: Brooklyn Dodgers → LA Dodgers (1958); NY Giants → SF Giants (1958); Montreal Expos → Washington Nationals (2005)
- **League switches**: Milwaukee Brewers: AL (1969–1997) → NL (1998–present)
- **Division structure**:
  - No divisions (1876–1968)
  - 2 divisions: East & West (1969)
  - 3 divisions + Wild Card (1994 operational in 1995)
- **Interleague play**: Began 1997; full schedule adopted 2023

## Data Quality
- Pre-1900 records may reflect different rules (no foul territory limits, pitching distances varied)
- All-time franchise totals include years in both leagues where applicable
- Win totals in September call-ups / roster expansion eras (post-1961) are not adjusted

## Update Schedule
- Annual update after World Series complete
- Key milestones tracked: pennants, WS titles, franchise record-breaking seasons
