## Era Championship Trends
- Pre-Modern (1876-1899): White Stockings lead with 6 pennants
- Dead Ball (1900-1919): Pirates/Cubs dominant
- Live Ball (1920-1945): Cardinals 10 pennants; Giants 14
- Post-War (1946-1968): Dodgers 12 pennants; Cardinals 6
- Expansion (1969-1992): Reds 5 pennants; Braves dynasty starts
- Divisional (1993-2019): Braves 14 straight division titles
- Modern (2020-): Dodgers 8 of last 10 NL pennants
