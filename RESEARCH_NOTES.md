## Data Validation Notes

1. **Franchise continuity**: Team names use modern designations; relocation history tracked in all data files.
2. **Schedule length variation**: Pre-1884 seasons had 60-154 games; modern 162-game schedule standard from 1961+ (AL) and 1962+ (NL expansion).
3. **COVID-19 impact**: 2020 season was 60 games; 2021–2022 returned to 162. Win% comparisons across eras should account for schedule length.
4. **Pitchers' mound height**: Changed from 15" to 10" in 1969, affecting offensive output.
5. **Draft**: Introduced 1965; long-term competitive impact on small-market teams.
