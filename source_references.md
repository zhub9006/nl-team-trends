### Championship & Era Highlights (2026 Research)
- Most NL pennants: LA Dodgers (26) through 2025 — includes Brooklyn era
- Most NL WS titles: St. Louis Cardinals (11)
- Most all-time NL wins: SF Giants (11,663)
- Most NL consecutive division titles: Atlanta Braves (14, 1991–2005)
- Most NL pennant drought: Cubs (108 years, 1908-2016)
- Recent NL dynasty: LA Dodgers (3 pennants 2020-2025, 8 straight NL West)
- Best single-season NL record: 1906 Cubs 116-36 (.763)
- Best 162-game NL record: 2022 Dodgers 111-51 (.685)
- Best shortened NL record: 2020 Dodgers 43-17 (.717)
- Only franchise expansion first-champion winner: AZ Diamondbacks 2001 (2nd year)
- NL teams in 2025 WS: Dodgers beat Toronto Blue Jays 4-3

### Data Conventions
- Win%: Wins / Games Played
- 162-game schedule adopted 1961; pre-1961 = 154 or fewer games
- Milwaukee Brewers moved from AL to NL Central in 1998
- Montreal Expos relocated to Washington Nationals in 2005
- 2020 season was 60 games (COVID-19)
- All-time records include pre-relocation franchise totals
- Pre-1903 pennants = NL championship only (no World Series existed)
- Split seasons: 1892 and 1981
- Interleague play: began 1997; full schedule since 2023
- 2026: ongoing season — NL stats reflect latest completed games (Cardinals 50-45, Brewers 59-37 through early July)
