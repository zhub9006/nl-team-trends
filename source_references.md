# Source References

Primary sources used for NL Team Trends research:

1. **Baseball-Reference (NL)** — https://www.baseball-reference.com/leagues/NL/ — Year-by-year NL standings & team stats (1876–present)
2. **Baseball Almanac** — https://www.baseball-almanac.com/teams/teamvsteam-nl.shtml — 15×15 H2H W-L matrices for every NL team vs every other team (1876–2026)
3. **Baseball Almanac (Year-by-Year)** — https://www.baseball-almanac.com/yearmenu.shtml — NL leadership, W-L records, and fabulous feats per season
4. **SABR Lahman Database** — https://sabr.org/lahman-database/ — Free downloadable CSV dataset (1871–2025) with full batting, pitching, and standings data
5. **Baseball Data Hub** — https://baseballdatahub.com/seasons/ — Complete season standings & stats archive (1871–2026)
6. **StatMuse** — https://www.statmuse.com/mlb — NL team W/L and franchise stats with query interface
7. **Wikipedia (NL Pennants)** — https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_National_League_pennant_winners — Complete pennant winner list with WS results
8. **Grokipedia** — https://grokipedia.com/page/List_of_all-time_Major_League_Baseball_win%E2%80%93loss_records — All-time MLB W-L records
9. **AP News** — https://apnews.com/article/new-york-los-angeles-pittsburgh-baseball-st-louis-eb7b9e9546a583a81b48f68480192151 — NL Championship Results
10. **Baseball Almanac (LCS History)** — https://www.baseball-almanac.com/League_Championship_Series.shtml — LCS results by year
11. **Everything Explained** — https://everything.explained.today — All-time franchise W-L & postseason data
12. **Linger & Look** — https://lingerandlook.com/Names/BaseballStandings.php — Year-by-year standings with managers & subtotals (1901–present)
13. **ESPN (World Series)** — https://www.espn.com/mlb/worldseries/history/winners — World Series champions & results by year
14. **StatsCrew (NL)** — https://www.statscrew.com/baseball/l-NL — NL rosters, standings & leaders (1876–present)
15. **Champs or Chumps** — https://champsorchumps.us/mlb — Win% rankings, droughts, streaks, postseason records
16. **MLB Win-Loss Visualizer** — https://inkandthunder.github.io/win-loss-visualizer/ — Interactive YoY W-L visualization (1894–present)
17. **BetIQ / TeamRankings** — https://betiq.teamrankings.com/mlb/betting-trends/win-loss-records/ — Every team's S/U W-L record with MOV and run-line data

## Data Credits
- Franchise continuity data: Adapted from Baseball Almanac and SABR Lahman Database
- H2H W-L matrices: Baseball Almanac (updated daily during regular season)
- Championship data: Cross-referenced from Baseball-Reference, Wikipedia, and ESPN
- 2025 season data: Simulated projections based on 2025 mid-season standings trends; verify with Baseball-Reference when season concludes
