## Data Methodology & Caveats

1. **Franchise continuity**: Relocations (Brooklyn→LA, NY Giants→SF, Montreal→Washington) INCLUDE all historical data. Chicago Cubs count includes Chicago White Stockings days (1876).
2. **H2H data**: Baseball Almanac includes H2H W-L matrices for all 15 NL teams vs all 15 NL teams (225 unique matchups). H2H summary file covers top rivalry matchups.
3. **Schedule eras**: Variable schedule lengths across eras (60–154 games pre-1962, 162 from 1962–present, 60 in 2020 COVID season). Win percentages are provided for cross-era comparison.
4. **Interleague play**: Beginning in 1997 and permanent since 2023. H2H data in this repo is NL-only; interleague results are tracked separately.
5. **Championship verification**: All results verified against ESPN World Series data and Baseball-Almanac records.
