### Era Transitions

- **1876-1900** (Founding Era): 8→12→8 teams; 60-112 game schedules; Boston, Chicago, Providence, Brooklyn dominant
- **1901-1920** (Early Expansion): Giants (6 pennants), Pirates (2), Cubs (2), Cardinals (2); Miracle Braves 1914; Cubs 1906-1908 historic wins
- **1920-1940** (Power Transition): Cardinals (5 pennants, establishing dynasty); NY Giants (4); Chicago Cubs (3); Gashouse Gang
- **1940-1960** (Postwar Modern): Cardinals (9 pennants); Dodgers/Giants rivalry; 1951 NYG; relocation Brooklyn→LA, NYG→SF
- **1960-1980** (Expansion): Dodgers (5); Reds (3); Mets (1); Padres/Expos/Rangers added; Miracle Mets; Big Red Machine
- **1981-2000** (Rivalries): Braves 14 straight division titles (1991-2005); Reds; dramatic competitive era
- **2001-2015** (Sentinel/Transition): Cardinals (3), Giants (3), D-backs (1), Mets (1), Braves (3); Cubs drought ends
- **2016-2025** (Modern Dodgers Dynasty): Dodgers (6 pennants, 8 straight NL West 2013-2020, 2022-2025); 9th WS title in 2025
